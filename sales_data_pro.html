Sales-Data-Analysis

In this project, I used Python, specifically the pandas and matplotlib libraries, to analyze and answer business questions based on 12 months of sales data. The dataset includes details on electronic store purchases, broken down by month, product type, cost, and purchase address.

Key Steps in the Analysis:

Data Cleaning: We started by cleaning the data. This involved:

  • Removing any empty (NaN) values.
  • Deleting rows based on specific conditions.
  • Changing column types to numeric or datetime formats as needed.

    • Data Exploration: After cleaning, we explored the data to answer four main business questions. This involved using various pandas and matplotlib techniques, such as:

  • Combining multiple CSV files into a single DataFrame using pd.concat.
  • Creating new columns by extracting information from strings using .str.
  • Grouping data for aggregate analysis with the .groupby() method.
  • Creating bar charts and line graphs to visualize the results and properly labeling them for clarity.
